BARKED QUESTIONS ANSWERED

What is Everyday™? +
Everyday™ is a daily supplement formulated for your dog’s overall health and well-being.
How do I know if my dog needs Everyday™? +
For wet foods, scoop the suggested serving amount and mix thoroughly. For dry foods, gently stir into your dog’s kibble. Consistency is key for best results.
At what age can my dog start using Everyday™? +
Everyday™ is safe for dogs 6 months and older. Always consult your vet before starting supplements for younger pups.
What does "100% Human-Grade" mean? +
It means all ingredients are the same quality as those used in food for humans, ensuring safety and high standards.
How many scoops are in each tube? +
Each tube contains approximately 30 scoops, enough for a full month’s supply for most dogs.
